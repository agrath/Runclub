About Us

We are an Auckland based running club created for the running enthusiast,
led by running enthusiasts.

We all share a love for the sport whether from the front or back of the pack
or somewhere in-between.

The message we support above all else is "enjoy your running and have fun".

Club membership is currently subscription free, we meet to train during the week and our free Sunday runs are popular, starting from various locations around Auckland and ending with coffee, carrot cake and a chat.
