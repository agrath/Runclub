Auckland Sunday Group Runs

Every Sunday at 8am join a small army of runners to explore Auckland’s most beautiful spots on foot.
Join our Auckland Sunday running group!

These social runs are totally free-of-charge and ideal for EVERY level of runner. Plus, you don’t have to be a club member.
At every run we have options to complete just 5k all the way up to 30k +.

Our pace leaders guide groups at the following paces: 4:30, 5, 5:30, 6, & 6:30 min/km (we’re currently looking for a 7:00 min/km leader).
So find a group that works for you and enjoy discovering Auckland’s best runs with great company.

And we’re on rain, hail or shine.

NOTE: All runners run at their own risk and agree not to hold the club or any run leaders responsible for any injury or damages to their person or belongings.
